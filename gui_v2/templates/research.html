What does each status mean?

Monitor — Strong enough to keep watching. Corroboration, news relevance, and persistence all met the governance gates. Still sandbox research; no investment action implied.

Needs Review — Mixed evidence; the operator should inspect. Some gates passed but not all.

Rejected — Risk or weak evidence. Either the risk-flag count exceeded the maximum, the candidate was already in the rejected list, or upstream carried a forbidden status.

Expired — No longer supported by recent evidence. No discovery memory signal within the staleness window.

This layer is capped at context_only and cannot alter portfolio, watchlist, allocation, scoring, recommendation, or decision state.
